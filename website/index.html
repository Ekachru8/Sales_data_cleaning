A rigorous, multi-step pipeline transforming raw messy data into analysis-ready format.

04

Enforced proper dtypes — datetime for dates, int for quantities, float for prices, string for IDs.

Key Findings

Business Insights

5 actionable, data-backed conclusions with strategic recommendations for business growth.

📅
Insight #1

Q4 Drives Disproportionate Revenue

Q4 accounts for a massive share of annual revenue, with November being the peak month driven by pre-Christmas gifting demand. This seasonal pattern is consistent across both years in the dataset.

→ Recommendation: Increase inventory and marketing spend by 40–50% in September to prepare for Q4 demand surge. Seasonal promotions are critical.
👥
Insight #2

Severe Customer Concentration Risk

The top 20% of customers generate the vast majority of total revenue, following a classic Pareto distribution. Losing even a few top customers could significantly impact the bottom line.

→ Recommendation: Implement a loyalty/retention program for top-tier customers while diversifying the revenue base through acquisition campaigns.
🌍
Insight #3

Heavy Geographic Dependency

The United Kingdom dominates the revenue share at ~82%, creating significant single-market risk. The next largest markets (Netherlands, EIRE, Germany) contribute comparatively small shares.

→ Recommendation: Accelerate international expansion in top-performing EU markets (DACH region, Netherlands, France) to reduce single-market risk.
⏰
Insight #4

Clear Peak Trading Windows

Analysis reveals distinct daily and hourly patterns — certain weekdays drive significantly more revenue, and orders peak during late morning to early afternoon hours.

→ Recommendation: Schedule promotions, email campaigns, and flash sales during peak windows. Ensure customer support staffing aligns with high-traffic hours.
📦
Insight #5

Long-Tail Product Portfolio

A small number of bestselling products contribute outsized revenue out of thousands of total SKUs. The long tail of low-performing products adds complexity without proportional revenue.

→ Recommendation: Focus marketing on proven bestsellers. Evaluate low-performing SKUs for discontinuation to reduce warehousing costs and inventory complexity.
